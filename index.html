The Man, The Myth

Who Is Nathan?

Nathan Waller is the kind of bloke who walks into a room and immediately makes it funnier — and no, that's not just because he walks like he's late to deliver important news.

On the surface: loud laughs, brilliant banter, the sort of company you'd want on a long drive or a rainy afternoon. Beneath that? A man with a deep faith, a quietly nerdy soul, and an encyclopaedic knowledge of both Scripture and soup varieties.

He'll debate theology with you over a beef dinner, then pivot to asking whether you've ever truly listened to Sweet Caroline. (He has. Multiple times. He considers it essential listening.)

New York is on the bucket list. Church is on the weekly schedule. And finding the right lass? Well — that's why you're here.
